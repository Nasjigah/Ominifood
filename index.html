All About Coffee

Coffee preparation is the process of turning coffee beans into a beverage. While the particular steps vary with the type of coffee and with the raw materials, the process includes four basic steps; raw coffee beans must be roasted, the roasted coffee beans must then be grinded, the grinded coffee must then be mixed with hot water for a certain time (brewed) and finally the liquid coffee must be separated from the used grounds.
